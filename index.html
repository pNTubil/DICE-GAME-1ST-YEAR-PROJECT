RULES:

The game is played in rounds by two players. A player may roll the two dice as many times as he or she wishes during each turn. Each result is added to the player's round score, but if the player rolls a 1 on either of the two dice, the entire round score is lost. Then it's the turn of the next player. The player can choose to "Hold," which adds his or her round score to his or her global score. Then it's the turn of the next player. The first player to reach 100 points or the game's points limit wins.
